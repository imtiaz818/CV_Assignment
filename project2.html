Project Introduction

CCT Connect is a social interactive website built for the students of CCT. The main functions of the website are as follows:
1-A chat system to allow registered students to join in a group chat to communicate with other users.
2-A quiz section based on general knowledge multi choice questions.
3-A library section containing video tutorials and books on a number of the modules covered in the course.
4-An entertainment section, which will help users keep up to date with CCTs upcoming social events, as well as all the latest music, movies and sports news.
5-A live news feed, running on all pages to keep students up to date on the newly added contents on the website.
